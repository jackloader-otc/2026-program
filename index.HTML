About us

The Global Fast-Food IconFounded in 1955 by Ray Kroc, McDonald's Corporation has evolved from a single Illinois franchise restaurant into the world's leading global foodservice retailer. Known for its revolutionary fast-food system, the brand has become a cultural icon recognized by its Golden Arches and legendary menu items like the Big Mac, Quarter Pounder, and world-famous French fries. Operating tens of thousands of locations across the globe, the company serves millions of customers daily by blending incredible speed, consistent quality, and unmatched convenience.

Innovation and Community ImpactModern McDonald's locations focus heavily on digital innovation and sustainability to meet changing consumer needs. Through the MyMacca's Rewards app, mobile ordering, and expanded multi-lane drive-thrus, the company delivers a highly customized and seamless customer experience. Beyond serving fast food, McDonald's prioritizes responsible sourcing and supports local communities. It provides vital funding for initiatives like Ronald McDonald House Charities to support families in need.
